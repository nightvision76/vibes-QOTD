📋 Connect Your Google Sheet

1. Open your Google Sheet and go to File → Share → Publish to web
2. Choose your sheet, select Comma-separated values (.csv), and click Publish
3. Copy the URL and paste it into the SHEET_CSV_URL variable at the top of this file's <script> section

Your sheet should have a column called question (row 1 = header).
